Favorite Spots

These are some of my favorite spots in NYC. You should definitely go to them!

  • Food Gallery 32 in Manhattan
    • A lot of options to choose from. My go-to food is the taiyaki near the front. This is their website
  • McDonalds near DeKalb Ave
    • Convenient, delicious, and cheap(ish). What's not to love?!?!

  • Starbucks (any location)
